Hi, I am

Ahmed Bayugo Latifa.

Final Year CS Student & Aspiring Researcher

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at the University of Energy and Natural Resources in Sunyani, Ghana. Four years in, I've moved from wondering how algorithms work to actually building systems that do things — and now I'm hungry for the research questions nobody has answered yet.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning and real-world problems. I'm drawn to questions about how we build systems that don't just perform well on benchmarks, but that are robust, fair, and meaningful in practice — especially in contexts that are often overlooked by mainstream research.

I read papers for fun, get unreasonably excited about a good visualisation, and believe that some of the most important AI research will come from places like Ghana. I'm currently seeking fully funded PhD/MS opportunities at US universities for Spring or Fall 2027.
